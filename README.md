# Agentic-Trader

A Gen AI powered autonomous trading system designed to analyze market dynamics, synthesize complex financial data, and execute intelligent investment strategies in real-time.

## Overview

Agentic-Trader is an intelligent investment platform that combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with financial market analysis to provide real-time trading recommendations and autonomous execution capabilities. The system processes vast amounts of market data and alternative data sources to identify promising investment opportunities and manage trading positions intelligently.

## Core Features

### 1. Real-Time Data Ingestion

Agentic-Trader continuously ingests and processes multiple data streams to maintain a comprehensive market view:

- **Stock Price Data**: Real-time and historical stock price feeds with minute-level granularity for technical analysis
- **Technical Indicators**: Automatic calculation and tracking of essential indicators including:
  - Moving averages (SMA, EMA)
  - Relative Strength Index (RSI)
  - MACD and Bollinger Bands
  - Volume-weighted indicators
  - Support and resistance levels

- **Global News Feed**: Integration with financial news sources to capture market-moving events and corporate announcements

- **Social Media Sentiment Analysis**: Real-time monitoring of social platforms to gauge investor sentiment, retail trading trends, and emerging market themes

### 2. Intelligent Stock Prediction & Analysis

The AI engine analyzes multi-dimensional data to predict optimal trading opportunities:

- **Stock Selection**: Predictive models identify stocks with the highest probability of positive returns
- **Sentiment Integration**: Combines quantitative technical analysis with qualitative sentiment data for holistic decision-making
- **Trend Detection**: Advanced pattern recognition identifies emerging market trends before they become mainstream
- **Risk Assessment**: Calculates risk-adjusted opportunity scores for each potential trade

### 3. Autonomous Trading Execution

Agentic-Trader can autonomously execute trades across multiple timeframes:

- **Intra-Day Trading**: Execute rapid buy-sell cycles to capitalize on short-term price movements within a single trading session
- **Short-Term Trading**: Manage positions over days to weeks based on technical and sentiment signals
- **Long-Term Investing**: Build and maintain strategic positions for weeks to months with fundamental analysis integration
- **Automated Portfolio Management**: Rebalance positions based on market conditions and risk thresholds
- **Risk Management**: Implement stop-loss, take-profit, and position-sizing rules automatically

### 4. Mutual Fund Recommendations

Beyond stock trading, the system provides intelligent fund allocation advice:

- **Long-Term Portfolio Construction**: Recommend diversified mutual fund allocations aligned with investor goals
- **Fund Performance Analysis**: Track and evaluate mutual fund performance against benchmarks
- **Asset Class Allocation**: Optimize portfolio allocation across equities, debt, and hybrid funds
- **Risk-Return Profile Matching**: Suggest funds matching investor risk tolerance and investment horizon

## How It Works

1. **Data Collection**: Continuously monitors stock prices, technical indicators, news feeds, and social media sentiment
2. **Analysis**: AI models analyze patterns, correlations, and anomalies across all data sources
3. **Decision Making**: Generates buy/sell signals with confidence scores and risk metrics
4. **Execution**: Autonomously executes trades or provides recommendations based on configured settings
5. **Monitoring**: Tracks position performance and adjusts strategies based on market evolution

## Use Cases

- **Active Traders**: Automate technical analysis and capture intra-day trading opportunities
- **Long-Term Investors**: Receive intelligent stock and mutual fund recommendations
- **Portfolio Managers**: Leverage AI-driven insights for portfolio construction and rebalancing
- **Retail Investors**: Access institutional-grade trading strategies without requiring deep market expertise

## Technology Stack

Agentic-Trader leverages state-of-the-art AI and data processing technologies to deliver real-time insights and autonomous trading capabilities.
